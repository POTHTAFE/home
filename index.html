About Our Chapter

TAFE (Texas Association of Future Educators) is a student organization that empowers aspiring educators through leadership, service, and professional development. Our chapter is committed to building a strong community of future teachers and leaders.

How to Get Involved

Whether you're a member or an officer, there are many ways to participate: attend meetings, volunteer at events, and contribute ideas. We welcome your energy and creativity!

Need Help?

If you have questions or suggestions, reach out to any officer or use the suggestion form on the Member Dashboard. We're here to support you!
